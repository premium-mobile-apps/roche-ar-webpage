Cobas 5800 WebAR Viewer

Innovation made accessible

Roche has created a new web-based tool for you to seamlessly view and interact with a full-scale digital twin 3D model of the Cobas 5800 directly in your space. Roche remains committed to supporting laboratories in meeting both their own needs and the needs of the patients everywhere.

How to interact with the WebAR Viewer

Instructions for Desktop

Click the 'AR' Icon. Scan the QR Code with a smartphone.

  • Rotate: Left-click and drag your mouse cursor along the scene canvas
  • Pan: Right-click and drag your mouse cursor
  • Zoom: Scroll your mouse
  • Quick Zoom: Click mouse wheel and drag the mouse
  • Change View: 'TAB' key to cycle between orthogonal views (front, top, right, left, back, bottom)
  • Fit view: 'A' key (helps you quickly fit all objects in the scene, if nothing is selected)

Instructions for Mobile

Tap the 'AR' Icon.

  • Rotate: Touch model with two fingers and rotate both fingers
  • Pan: Touch and hold 3D model with a finger and drag around your space
  • Fit view: Double tap 3D model with a finger
